## Relevant Theory

See [[semiotics]] and [[narrative]] for the theoretical grounding.

Key figures: [[saussure]], [[peirce]], [[barthes]], [[lacan]], [[derrida]], [[eco]], [[propp]], [[greimas]]

The most related fields are:
- **Semiotics** — how things mean things
- **Structuralism** — how pieces fit together in stories

NLP tools (POS tagging, sentiment scoring) handle some of the mechanical work. What Plotz concentrates on is *narrative* — making it granular, then building back up into different kinds of pieces.

## Note on Structuralism

Much of this academic field uses elaborate philosophical scaffolding to arrive at the conclusion that narrative data should be represented as a graph. We're doing that.
