# Semiotics

How meaning is made from signs. The foundational lens for narrative analysis.

The books seem to focus on Saussure/Peirce, and then move chronologically through structuralism and post-structuralism. Gather topics, organize them, work through the ones that make sense.

## Key Figures
- [[plato]] — earliest theories of representation and mimesis
- [[st-augustine]] — signs and signification in theological context
- [[saussure]] — signifier/signified, arbitrary nature of the sign
- [[peirce]] — icon/index/symbol triadic model
- [[barthes]] — myth, connotation, the death of the author
- [[lacan]] — the unconscious structured like a language
- [[derrida]] — deconstruction, différance, the instability of meaning
- [[eco]] — semiotics of culture, open/closed texts
