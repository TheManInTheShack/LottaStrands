# Plato

Greek philosopher (428–348 BCE).

## Key ideas (relevant to semiotics)
- **Mimesis** — representation/imitation; art as copy of a copy of the Form
- **The Cratylus** — dialogue on whether names are natural or conventional; anticipates the Saussurean debate
- **Forms and particulars** — the original split between signified and referent

## Relevance
The earliest systematic treatment of representation. The mimesis problem (what is the relationship between a text and what it depicts?) is still the foundational question for narrative analysis.
