# Jacques Derrida

French philosopher (1930–2004). Founder of deconstruction.

## Key ideas
- **Différance** — meaning is always deferred; no final signified
- **Deconstruction** — binary oppositions are unstable; the suppressed term haunts the dominant one
- **Trace** — every sign carries traces of what it excludes
- **There is nothing outside the text** (*il n'y a pas de hors-texte*)
