# Augustine of Hippo

North African theologian (354–430 CE).

## Key ideas (relevant to semiotics)
- **De Doctrina Christiana** — systematic theory of signs in a theological context; one of the earliest explicit sign theories
- **Signum** — anything that causes something else to come to mind; anticipates modern sign theory
- **Natural vs. conventional signs** — the distinction Saussure would later formalize

## Relevance
A bridge between ancient philosophy and medieval/modern sign theory. Notable as an early example of treating the sign relationship as something that can be studied systematically.
