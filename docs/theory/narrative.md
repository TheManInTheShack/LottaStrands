## Narrative vs. History

Two threads running through any story:

- **Narrative** (blue thread) — what the text says, in the order it says it. Has a POV. Can skip, contradict, falsify. 100% present in the work.
- **History** (green thread) — the events as they "actually" happened in the story world. Objective in principle, mostly unknown, contradictions recordable but not resolvable.
- **Real world** (brown thread) — the context in which the work was produced and received.

The relationship between these threads is where the interesting analysis lives. See [[pov]].
